About Us

Located between Edinburgh and Carlisle, this picturesque small farm has been lovingly transformed into a welcoming visitor attraction.

The farm now features a charming shop where guests can purchase fresh, locally sourced produce and beautiful plants.

For animal lovers, there’s a delightful petting zoo offering hands-on experiences with friendly farm animals. Visitors can also relax at the cozy café, enjoy a leisurely stroll along scenic walking trails, or let children burn off energy in the fun play park.

Whether you're looking to explore, shop, or simply unwind, this inviting farm offers something for everyone in a peaceful countryside setting.
